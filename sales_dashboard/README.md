# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

Interactive Power BI dashboard providing executive-level insights into e-commerce performance.

## Overview

Analysis of **£17.74M in revenue** across **36,98K orders** from **5,881 customers** (2010-2011 data).

## Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue:** £17.74M
- **Average Order Value:** £479.88
- **Total Orders:** 36.98K
- **Total Customers:** 5,881

## Insights

- **Seasonal Revenue Pattern:** Clear Q4 spike driven by holiday shopping (November peak at £1.2M)
- **Market Concentration:** UK dominates revenue generation, accounting for majority of sales
- **Product Mix:** Top sellers are decorative/gift items (Regency Cakestand, Hanging Heart designs)
- **Business Trend:** Revenue declined through 2011, indicating potential market or operational challenges

## Features

- Executive KPI cards showing key business metrics at a glance
- Monthly revenue trend line revealing seasonality patterns
- Top 5 markets by revenue (geographic analysis)
- Best-selling products table with quantity and revenue breakdown
