# Customer Segmentation Analysis - E-Commerce

## Project Overview
RFM-based customer segmentation analysis of 1M+ e-commerce transactions to identify high-value customers and growth opportunities.

## Key Findings
- 31% of customers (VIP segment) generate 75% of revenue
- Price is NOT the primary churn driver for at-risk customers
- £1.27M+ revenue opportunity identified through targeted strategies

## Tools & Techniques
- **Language:** Python 3.x
- **Libraries:** pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn
- **Methods:** RFM segmentation, exploratory data analysis, customer behavior analysis
- **Dataset:** Online Retail Dataset (2009-2011, ~1M transactions)

## Business Impact
- Identified 6 distinct customer segments with tailored recommendations
- Quantified revenue preservation opportunities (£525K+ VIP retention)
- Discovered undermonetized segment with £137K growth potential

## Files
- `notebook.ipynb` - Full analysis with code and visualizations
